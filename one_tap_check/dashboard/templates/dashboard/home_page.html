About Us

OneTap Check is an innovative attendance management system that utilizes Arduino technology. Our system allows educators and organizations to efficiently track attendance using a tapping mechanism.

By integrating Arduino devices with our software, users can easily record attendance with just a tap. Our system provides real-time insights and analytics to help optimize attendance tracking processes.

System Parts

The Arduino device is the heart of the OneTap Check system. It serves as the central controller responsible for managing attendance data. With its wireless communication capabilities, it interacts seamlessly with RFID tags and other peripherals. Housed in a protective case, it ensures durability and stability.

The Arduino device is the central component of the OneTap Check system. It serves as the main controller responsible for managing attendance data. The Arduino is equipped with wireless communication capabilities, allowing it to interact with RFID tags and other peripherals. Additionally, it can be housed in a protective case to ensure durability and stability.
RFID (Radio-Frequency Identification) technology is used to uniquely identify objects or individuals wirelessly. In the OneTap Check system, RFID tags are assigned to users and are tapped on the Arduino device to record attendance. These tags operate at a frequency of 13.56MHz, providing fast and reliable communication with the RC522 module.
The RC522 module serves as the intermediary between the RFID tags and the Arduino board. It facilitates communication by reading data from the tags and transmitting it to the Arduino for processing.
